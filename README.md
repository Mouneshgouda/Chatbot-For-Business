This syllabus is designed for **BCA students with little to no AI knowledge**. The course focuses on **practical, hands-on learning** with minimal theory. By the end of the course, students will be able to build simple AI chatbots for business use and understand basic automation.

---

## Week 1: Introduction to Chatbots and Business Use

### Theory (Very Basic)
- What is a chatbot?  
- Difference: Rule-based vs AI chatbot  
- Why businesses use chatbots:
  - Customer support  
  - Lead collection  
  - Sales assistant  

### Practical
- Students pick a business type (restaurant, coaching, online store)  
- Draw a simple chatbot flow on paper:
  1. Greeting  
  2. Ask a question  
  3. Collect contact  
  4. Give answer  

---

## Week 2: Build Your First Chatbot (No Coding)

### Tool Options (Free / No Coding)
- ManyChat  
- Tidio  

### Hands-On
- Create a welcome message  
- Add a simple FAQ section  
- Add a contact form  

**Outcome:** Each student has a working chatbot on a website or Facebook page.  

---

## Week 3: Make It Smarter with AI (No Coding)

### Tool Options
- OpenAI API (or ManyChat AI integration)  

### Hands-On
- Add AI to answer questions naturally  
- Give AI a friendly business tone  
- Test chatbot for:
  - FAQs  
  - Booking  
  - Customer queries  

**Outcome:** Students have a chatbot that can answer like a human agent.  

---

## Week 4: Add Knowledge Base (Optional Advanced Step)

### Goal
Chatbot answers from business documents.  

### Practical
- Upload PDFs or FAQ documents to AI chatbot  
- Test answers from uploaded documents  

**Outcome:** Chatbot knows company information without manual coding.  

---

## Week 5: Connect to Tools / Automation

### Tool Options
- Zapier  

### Hands-On
- Save user responses to Google Sheet  
- Send email notifications automatically  
- Notify admin when a user requests a call  

**Outcome:** Chatbot works like a mini automated system.  

---

## Week 6: Mini Project + Presentation

### Project Options
- Restaurant order chatbot  
- Real estate lead bot  
- Coaching enquiry chatbot  
- E-commerce support bot  

### Deliverables
- Live working chatbot  
- Short report: business idea + flow  
- Demo presentation to class  

### Optional
- Teach basic freelancing idea: offer chatbot setup service for small businesses  

---

## Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:
- Build real AI chatbots for business purposes  
- Understand chatbot automation and integration  
- Prepare a portfolio for freelancing or internship opportunities  
- Present and demonstrate their chatbot projects
